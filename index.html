Campaigns

These are the campaigns I run to grow communities that are actually active, not just big numbers on a dashboard.

01
Ambassador Program
You find people who genuinely love your project and give them a reason to talk about it. They post, recruit, and represent you in communities you would never reach alone.
Projects pick this because real people recommending a project will always beat a paid ad.
02
Referral Campaigns
You reward your current community for bringing in new members. Simple mechanic: bring a friend, get something valuable back.
Projects pick this because people who join through a friend are more likely to stay and actually care about the project.
03
Quest / Task Campaigns
You give your community specific things to do: follow, share, swap, hold. They complete tasks and earn rewards. Platforms like Galxe or Layer3 make it easy to track everything.
Projects pick this because every completed quest is a real action you can measure.
04
AMAs
The founder or team goes live and answers questions directly from the community. Setup, promotion, and question curation all handled so the session runs smoothly.
Projects pick this because nothing builds trust faster than a founder speaking openly to their community.
05
Zealy Campaigns
You build a mission board on Zealy where community members complete tasks, earn XP, and compete on a leaderboard.
Projects pick this because it turns your community into an active group instead of a quiet Telegram chat.
06
Gleam Campaigns
You run a giveaway where people enter by following accounts, retweeting, or signing up. Fast to set up and easy to track.
Projects pick this because it creates a quick burst of reach right when you need it, like around a launch or a big announcement.
